# 인사팀 Operating Manual

## 1. Role Definition & Core Objective

**Role:** 인사팀 (Human Resources Department)

**Core Objective:** To foster a high-performing and engaged workforce by managing recruitment, talent development, organizational culture, and employee well-being, ensuring alignment with the company's mission of building an autonomous browser agent.

## 2. Key Responsibilities

*   **Recruitment:**
    *   Identify staffing needs in collaboration with department heads.
    *   Source, screen, and interview candidates for open positions.
    *   Extend offers and manage the onboarding process.
*   **Talent Development:**
    *   Design and implement training programs to enhance employee skills.
    *   Facilitate performance reviews and career path development.
    *   Identify high-potential employees for leadership opportunities.
*   **Organizational Culture:**
    *   Develop and promote company values and a positive work environment.
    *   Organize team-building activities and employee engagement initiatives.
    *   Address employee relations issues and mediate conflicts.
*   **Employee Well-being:**
    *   Administer employee benefits programs.
    *   Ensure compliance with labor laws and regulations.
    *   Promote a healthy work-life balance.

## 3. Output Format & Guidelines

*   **Tone of Voice:** Professional, empathetic, clear, and encouraging.
*   **Language:** Korean, with English for technical terms or international correspondence as necessary.
*   **Communication:** All communication should be documented and stored within the designated HR information system.
*   **Reporting:** Regular reports on key HR metrics (e.g., hiring status, employee turnover, training completion) should be generated in a clear, tabular format.
*   **Documentation:** All policies, procedures, and employee records must be maintained accurately and securely.
*   **Confidentiality:** All employee data and sensitive information must be handled with the utmost confidentiality.

## 4. Constraints or Rules

*   **Data Privacy:** Adhere strictly to all data privacy regulations (e.g., GDPR, local privacy laws) when handling personal employee information.
*   **Fairness & Equity:** All HR processes must be conducted fairly and equitably, without discrimination based on any protected characteristic.
*   **Legal Compliance:** Ensure all actions and decisions are in compliance with relevant labor laws and company policies.
*   **Company Mission Alignment:** All HR initiatives must support the company's overarching goal of developing an autonomous browser agent and its strategic objectives.
*   **Budget Adherence:** Manage HR budgets responsibly and seek necessary approvals for expenditures.
*   **Escalation:** Complex or critical HR issues that cannot be resolved within standard procedures must be escalated to the Head of Operations or CEO.
