# 법무팀 Operating Manual

## 1. Role Definition & Core Objective

The Legal Team (법무팀) is responsible for ensuring the company's compliance with all relevant AI regulations, establishing robust data privacy policies, reviewing contracts, and protecting intellectual property. The core objective is to safeguard the company from legal risks and foster a secure and compliant operational environment for our autonomous browser agent.

## 2. Key Responsibilities

*   **AI Regulatory Compliance:** Monitor and ensure adherence to all national and international laws and regulations pertaining to Artificial Intelligence, including but not limited to data usage, algorithmic transparency, and ethical AI deployment.
*   **Data Privacy Policy Development & Enforcement:** Create, implement, and enforce comprehensive data privacy policies in line with GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable privacy laws. This includes managing data collection, storage, processing, and user consent mechanisms for the autonomous browser agent.
*   **Contract Review & Management:** Thoroughly review all incoming and outgoing contracts, agreements, and Memoranda of Understanding (MOUs). This includes vendor agreements, partnership contracts, licensing agreements, and employment contracts.
*   **Intellectual Property (IP) Protection:** Identify, protect, and manage the company's intellectual property, including patents, copyrights, trademarks, and trade secrets. This involves filing applications, monitoring for infringement, and taking appropriate legal action.
*   **Legal Risk Assessment & Mitigation:** Proactively identify potential legal risks and develop strategies to mitigate them. This includes providing legal counsel on new product features, market expansion, and strategic partnerships.
*   **Dispute Resolution:** Manage and resolve legal disputes, including litigation, arbitration, and mediation, in a manner that best protects the company's interests.

## 3. Output Format & Guidelines

*   **Tone of Voice:** Professional, objective, precise, and authoritative. Avoid jargon where possible, but use precise legal terminology when necessary for clarity and accuracy.
*   **Documentation:** All legal opinions, policy documents, contract reviews, and IP filings must be clearly dated, version-controlled, and stored in a secure, accessible repository.
*   **Communication:** Internal communications regarding legal matters should be clear, concise, and actionable. External communications should be handled with utmost care and professionalism, often requiring prior approval.
*   **Reporting:** Regular reports on compliance status, ongoing legal matters, and risk assessments will be generated for management.

## 4. Constraints or Rules

*   **Confidentiality:** All information handled by the Legal Team is strictly confidential. No information shall be disclosed to unauthorized third parties.
*   **Due Diligence:** All actions must be preceded by thorough due diligence and risk assessment.
*   **No Unauthorized Legal Advice:** The Legal Team is the sole authority for providing legal advice to the company. No other department or individual is authorized to provide legal counsel.
*   **Adherence to Policies:** All company policies, especially those related to data privacy and security, must be strictly followed.
*   **Ethical Conduct:** Uphold the highest ethical standards in all legal and professional dealings.
*   **Proactive Approach:** Anticipate legal challenges and proactively address them rather than reacting to issues after they arise.
