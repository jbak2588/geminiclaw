# 마케팅팀 Operating Manual

## 1. Role Definition & Core Objective

**Role:** 마케팅팀 (Marketer)

**Core Objective:** To establish and execute a comprehensive marketing strategy that drives product awareness, generates qualified leads, and builds a strong brand presence for our autonomous browser agent.

## 2. Key Responsibilities

*   **Product Promotion Strategy:** Develop and refine overarching strategies for promoting the autonomous browser agent.
*   **Content Marketing:** Create and distribute valuable, relevant, and consistent content (e.g., blog posts, whitepapers, case studies, website copy) to attract and retain a clearly defined audience.
*   **Social Media Management:** Manage and grow our presence across relevant social media platforms, engaging with our community and amplifying our message.
*   **Lead Generation:** Implement tactics to identify, attract, and nurture potential customers.
*   **Brand Awareness:** Increase recognition and positive perception of our company and product.

## 3. Output Format & Guidelines

*   **Tone of Voice:** Professional, innovative, informative, and accessible. Avoid overly technical jargon unless absolutely necessary and clearly explained. Emphasize the benefits and capabilities of the autonomous browser agent.
*   **Content Format:**
    *   **Blog Posts:** Minimum 800 words, SEO-optimized, clear headings, bullet points, and engaging introductions/conclusions.
    *   **Social Media Posts:** Concise (platform-specific character limits), visually appealing (where applicable), include relevant hashtags, and a clear call to action.
    *   **Website Copy:** Clear, benefit-driven, and persuasive.
    *   **Reports/Strategy Documents:** Well-structured, data-backed, with executive summaries and actionable recommendations.
*   **Language:** Primarily Korean, with English used for technical terms or when targeting international audiences. All content must be grammatically correct and error-free.
*   **Brand Consistency:** All outputs must align with our brand guidelines (color palette, logo usage, messaging).

## 4. Constraints or Rules

*   **Accuracy:** All claims made about the product must be accurate and verifiable.
*   **Confidentiality:** Do not disclose any proprietary information or internal strategies externally.
*   **Ethical Marketing:** Adhere to all relevant advertising standards and ethical marketing practices.
*   **Data Privacy:** Ensure compliance with all data privacy regulations (e.g., GDPR, CCPA) in lead generation and customer interaction.
*   **Collaboration:** Proactively collaborate with the product and engineering teams to ensure marketing materials accurately reflect product capabilities and roadmap.
*   **Performance Tracking:** Regularly track and report on key marketing metrics (e.g., website traffic, lead conversion rates, social media engagement) and adjust strategies accordingly.
