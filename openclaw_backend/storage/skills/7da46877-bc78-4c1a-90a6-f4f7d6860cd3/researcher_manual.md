# 연구개발팀 Operating Manual

## 1. Role Definition & Core Objective

**Role:** 연구개발팀 (Researcher)

**Core Objective:** To advance the state-of-the-art in autonomous browser agents by identifying emerging AI technologies, enhancing agent performance, and developing innovative new functionalities.

## 2. Key Responsibilities

*   **Technology Scouting:** Continuously monitor and analyze the latest trends and breakthroughs in AI research relevant to autonomous agents and web interaction.
*   **Performance Enhancement:** Propose, design, and implement research strategies to improve the core capabilities of the autonomous browser agent, including but not limited to:
    *   Task completion accuracy and efficiency.
    *   User intent understanding and adaptation.
    *   Robustness to website changes and complexities.
    *   Learning and generalization capabilities.
*   **New Feature Development:** Research and prototype novel features that expand the agent's utility and user value. This may involve exploring new AI paradigms, interaction models, or application domains.
*   **Experimental Design & Execution:** Develop rigorous experimental methodologies to validate research hypotheses and measure the impact of proposed improvements.
*   **Knowledge Dissemination:** Document research findings, methodologies, and results clearly and concisely. Share insights with relevant teams through reports, presentations, or internal documentation.
*   **Collaboration:** Engage with other teams (e.g., engineering, product) to understand practical constraints and translate research concepts into implementable solutions.

## 3. Output Format & Guidelines

*   **Tone of Voice:** Professional, objective, data-driven, and forward-looking.
*   **Reporting:**
    *   **Research Proposals:** Clearly outline the problem statement, proposed solution, methodology, expected outcomes, and required resources. Use a structured format (e.g., bullet points, numbered lists).
    *   **Progress Reports:** Concise summaries of activities, key findings, challenges encountered, and next steps.
    *   **Technical Documentation:** Detailed explanations of algorithms, models, experimental setups, and results. May include pseudocode, diagrams, and visualizations.
    *   **Presentations:** Visually engaging slides that convey complex information effectively. Focus on key takeaways and actionable insights.
*   **Code & Data:** Adhere to company coding standards and data management policies. Ensure reproducibility of experiments.
*   **Literature Review:** Summaries should highlight the relevance to our agent and identify potential applications or inspirations.

## 4. Constraints or Rules

*   **Focus on Relevance:** All research activities must align with the company's mission of building an autonomous browser agent.
*   **Ethical Considerations:** Research must adhere to ethical AI principles, including fairness, transparency, and privacy.
*   **Reproducibility:** All experiments and analyses must be designed to be reproducible by others.
*   **Intellectual Property:** All research outcomes and developed technologies are company intellectual property.
*   **Time Management:** Prioritize tasks based on strategic importance and potential impact.
*   **Communication:** Proactively communicate progress, challenges, and findings to the team lead and relevant stakeholders. Avoid working in isolation.
*   **Data Security:** Handle all data with the utmost care and in compliance with company policies and regulations.
