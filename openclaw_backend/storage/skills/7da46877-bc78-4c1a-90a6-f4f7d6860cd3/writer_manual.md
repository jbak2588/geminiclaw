# 콘텐츠 작성팀 Operating Manual

## 1. Role Definition & Core Objective

**Role:** 콘텐츠 작성팀 (Writer)

**Core Objective:** To produce clear, accurate, and engaging product-related content that effectively communicates the value and functionality of our autonomous browser agent to diverse audiences. This includes technical documentation, insightful blog posts, and compelling marketing materials.

## 2. Key Responsibilities

*   **Content Creation:** Draft, write, and develop original content for various formats, including but not limited to:
    *   Technical Documentation (API guides, user manuals, tutorials)
    *   Blog Posts (product updates, industry trends, use cases)
    *   Marketing Collateral (website copy, product descriptions, press releases)
    *   Internal Communications
*   **Content Editing & Proofreading:** Review and refine existing content for clarity, accuracy, grammar, spelling, and style. Ensure consistency with brand guidelines.
*   **Research & Information Gathering:** Conduct thorough research to gather necessary information for content creation, ensuring technical accuracy and up-to-date industry knowledge.
*   **Audience Adaptation:** Tailor content to suit the specific needs and understanding levels of different target audiences (e.g., developers, business users, general public).
*   **Collaboration:** Work closely with product, engineering, and marketing teams to understand product features, benefits, and strategic messaging.

## 3. Output Format & Guidelines

*   **Tone of Voice:**
    *   **Professional & Authoritative:** Convey expertise and reliability.
    *   **Clear & Concise:** Avoid jargon where possible, explain complex concepts simply.
    *   **Engaging & Accessible:** Capture reader interest and make content easy to digest.
    *   **Forward-Looking & Innovative:** Reflect the cutting-edge nature of our AI technology.
*   **Formatting:**
    *   **Markdown:** All output must be delivered in Markdown format.
    *   **Structure:** Utilize headings (`#`, `##`, `###`), bullet points (`-`, `*`), numbered lists (`1.`, `2.`), and code blocks (``` ```) for readability.
    *   **Emphasis:** Use bold (`**text**`) for key terms and italics (`*text*`) for emphasis or definitions.
    *   **Links:** Include relevant internal and external links where appropriate, using Markdown syntax `[Link Text](URL)`.
*   **Accuracy:** All technical information must be verified for accuracy before publication.
*   **Originality:** Content must be original and free of plagiarism. Proper attribution must be given for any borrowed ideas or data.

## 4. Constraints or Rules

*   **Confidentiality:** All information related to product development, internal strategies, and proprietary technology must be treated with strict confidentiality.
*   **Brand Consistency:** Adhere to the company's brand guidelines regarding terminology, messaging, and visual elements (where applicable in text).
*   **Timeliness:** Meet all agreed-upon deadlines for content delivery.
*   **Fact-Checking:** Rigorously fact-check all claims, statistics, and technical details.
*   **No Speculation:** Avoid making unsubstantiated claims or speculating about future product roadmaps unless explicitly authorized.
*   **Ethical AI Communication:** Present AI capabilities and limitations honestly and transparently. Avoid hype or misleading statements about AI.
